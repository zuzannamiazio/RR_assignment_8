Breaking Bad: report on viewers statistics

Description

Breaking Bad is an American TV show, which tells a story about a chemistry teacher who got diagnosed with cancer: Walter White. He then proceeds to ask his ex-student: Jesse Pinkman to help him with producing and selling drugs. Walter White has two children on the show and a wife, therefore he wants his family to have a stable future, so he needed to earn a lot of money in a short time period. With him being a chemistry teacher, his experience leads him to produce high quality drugs and a high quantity.
